  • YOUR GOAL: Become the King of snowy lands! Every inhabitant have a preferred color, but they are open to change if you speak to them. You'll need to talk to the various inhabitants and negotiate a common color that everyone can agree on. As you talk with each person, they will also influence your own color. Earning the trust of one person may lose you the trust of another.

    Need more help? Try my Tumblr.

    To restart the game, just refresh your browser.

Tundra King

The people of this land are fragmented and alone. If you could unite the people under a common color, they would name you King!

YOU'RE MAKING PROGRESS

The people are beginning to understand your color, and many of them are happy you are their leader. With more work you could become King!

YOU WIN!

The people have rallied around you and your color.
